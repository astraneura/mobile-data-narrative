What is Animal Crossing?

Animal Crossing: New Horizons (ACNH) was released on March 20, 2020, and was the fifth main entry in the Animal Crossing series.

The main story of the game begins with landing on a private, deserted island that you, the player, purchased a getaway trip to.

Upon landing, the game randomly assigns the island it's airport color, native fruit type, and starting villagers

As the game progresses, you meet new villagers and invite them to live on your island, collect insects, fish, and fossils for the museum exhibits, and upgrade your home.

This island would not be possible with the base designs; the circular ground designs don't exist as a premade option.

Part of the appeal of ACNH is the various decoration options for making an island customized to every player's preferred aesthetic.

Players often decorate the front of their island the most, because this area is shown when others join their island for co-op play.

Co-op also allows for more diverse decoration options, with players being able to share designs for flags, clothing, and paths.

This has led to far more creative designs than the base options allow, leading to some beautiful island designs.

This can lead to some players getting stuck in a cycle of resetting the island generation to get the perfect formula for their decorative plan.

So, Why This Topic?

I chose to focus my research on player's favorite personality type and favorite villagers, and also the the randomness of the game's starting random factors.

I chose this topic because I thought it would be interesting to see if a person's favorite personality type seemed to match their favorite villager, and I chose the second topic because I was interested in finding out how random the beginning factors were.

The questions I sought to answer were if someone's favorite villager would match their favorite personality type, and what the distribution of starting factors would be after I reset the game one hundred times.

I used a Google Survey and posted it to two subreddits, r/AnimalCrossing and r/ACNH, and asked for voluntary responses, and received 96 responses.
